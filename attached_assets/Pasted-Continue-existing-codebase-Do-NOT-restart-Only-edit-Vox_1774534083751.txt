LIGHTING SYSTEM OVERHAUL:

1. THREE-POINT LIGHTING SETUP
   
   Key light (main):
   Direction: top-left-front (-0.4, 0.8, 0.6)
   Intensity: 1.0
   
   Fill light (reduces harsh shadows):
   Direction: right-front (0.6, 0.3, 0.5)
   Intensity: 0.35
   
   Back light (rim/separation):
   Direction: behind-top (0, 0.5, -0.8)
   Intensity: 0.25
   
   Combined: 
   totalLight = keyIntensity * dot(normal, keyDir)
              + fillIntensity * dot(normal, fillDir)  
              + backIntensity * dot(normal, backDir)
   Clamp to 0.25 (min) to 1.4 (max)

2. FACE-SPECIFIC SHADING
   
   Forehead: +8% brightness (catches light)
   Cheeks: base brightness
   Under nose: -12% (nose shadow)
   Under chin: -18% (chin shadow)
   Eye sockets: -15% (recessed)
   
   Apply by modifying color before rendering
   based on voxel position in face region.

3. CLOTHING SHADING
   
   White tee:
   - Highlight area (chest center): #F8F8F8
   - Standard area: #F0F0F0
   - Side/shadow area: #D8D8D8
   - Deep fold areas: #C8C8C8
   - Armpit/underarm: #BCBCBC
   
   Black trousers:
   - Highlight (thigh front): #2A2A3A
   - Standard: #18181F
   - Shadow/inner leg: #0C0C12
   - Deep crease: #060608
   
   The difference between highlight and shadow
   must be clearly visible — this creates the
   premium voxel sculpture look.

4. VOXEL FACE SHADING (4 faces per cube)
   
   Current multipliers are too similar.
   New values:
   - Top face:    baseColor × 1.40  (brightest)
   - Front face:  baseColor × 1.00  (base)
   - Left face:   baseColor × 0.78
   - Right face:  baseColor × 0.62  (darker)
   - Bottom face: baseColor × 0.40  (darkest)
   
   This 1.4x to 0.4x range (3.5x contrast)
   creates the dramatic voxel cube appearance.

5. AMBIENT OCCLUSION SIMULATION
   
   Add darker voxels at concave joints:
   
   Neck-shoulder junction: darken 20%
   Armpit area: darken 25%
   Elbow inner: darken 15%
   Trouser crotch: darken 20%
   Behind knee: darken 18%
   Ankle-shoe join: darken 15%
   
   These "contact shadows" make the character
   feel grounded and 3-dimensional.

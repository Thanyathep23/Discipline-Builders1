3. identify the minimum high-impact fixes needed to make the app feel like a cohesive premium product
4. implement only the minimum set of changes needed
5. avoid broad refactors and avoid adding new systems

==================================================
A. CROSS-SYSTEM COHESION PASS
==================================================

Unify how the major product systems feel together.

Purpose:
The app should feel like one coherent Life RPG product, not separate modules.

Areas to align:
- mission language
- reward language
- progression language
- status tier language
- title/badge/asset language
- marketplace and world terminology
- guidance/recommendation terminology
- premium/upgrade terminology
- challenge/arc/chain/cycle terminology

Requirements:
- remove conflicting terms and duplicated concepts where practical
- standardize labels, status chips, and meaning across surfaces
- ensure item states such as owned / active / equipped / displayed / locked / premium / limited are consistent everywhere
- ensure mission states and progression states are presented consistently everywhere
- ensure reward explanation language feels unified across result views, marketplace, and identity/world surfaces

Do not rewrite the whole IA.
Focus on cohesion and clarity.

==================================================
B. UX FRICTION CLEANUP
==================================================

Reduce the most important moments of confusion, hesitation, or friction.

Audit and improve:
- first-time use of major surfaces
- transition from mission -> proof -> reward -> next step
- transition from reward -> store/world/status
- equip/apply/display flows
- category/store browsing friction
- recommendation visibility and trust
- circle/community visibility clutter if present
- premium/upgrade surfaces if they feel too detached or too salesy

Requirements:
- reduce unnecessary taps or hidden next steps where practical
- improve “what just happened?” clarity after important actions
- improve recovery after failed uploads, rejected proof, or interrupted flows
- improve discoverability of high-value screens without overwhelming users
- improve consistency of CTA placement and decision hierarchy

==================================================
C. VISUAL POLISH / INTERACTION POLISH
==================================================

Add targeted polish to make the product feel premium and complete.

Possible areas:
- better spacing and hierarchy
- stronger card consistency
- cleaner section grouping
- improved motion/microinteractions for key success moments
- stronger active/equipped/displayed states
- smoother transitions between status/world/store/reward flows
- better loading/skeleton/empty states
- cleaner feedback after actions like:
  - mission accepted
  - proof approved
  - item purchased
  - item equipped
  - world updated
  - title unlocked
  - mastery/prestige progression

Requirements:
- polish should reinforce clarity, not add noise
- keep motion tasteful and premium
- avoid over-animation
- focus polish on high-emotion and high-friction moments
- preserve the dark premium / command-center style

==================================================
D. PERFORMANCE / RENDERING OPTIMIZATION
==================================================

Optimize the most important performance hotspots.

Audit and improve where necessary:
- home/dashboard rendering
- status/character evolution rendering
- room/world rendering
- marketplace/catalog lists
- inventory/equip views
- heavy image/media usage
- admin/internal heavy tables if relevant
- repeated data fetching or unnecessary rerenders
- slow combined queries in high-value flows
- loading behavior for recommendation/guidance-heavy screens

Requirements:
- improve obvious bottlenecks
- use pagination/lazy loading where appropriate
- reduce oversized payloads where practical
- cache or memoize only where justified
- keep correctness and maintainability
- do not do speculative over-optimization

If useful, include lightweight performance notes for remaining known hotspots.

==================================================
E. STATE CONSISTENCY / INTEGRATION CLEANUP
==================================================

Ensure the major systems stay in sync and feel trustworthy.

Audit and improve consistency between:
- rewards and wallet state
- owned items and applied/equipped/displayed state
- character/status visual state and real progression data
- marketplace/storefront state and inventory state
- profile/showcase state and world/room state
- recommendations and current user state
- guidance cards and actual available actions
- circle/shared visibility and actual current profile/world identity
- premium entitlements and gated surfaces

Requirements:
- fix any stale-state or contradictory-state UX
- ensure post-action refreshes are coherent
- keep server-side authority intact
- preserve JSON-only APIs and ownership checks
- if small recompute/reconciliation helpers are needed, keep them minimal and safe

==================================================
F. INFORMATION HIERARCHY / PRODUCT CLARITY
==================================================

Because the product is now deep, improve clarity of what matters most.

Requirements:
- strengthen “what matters now” hierarchy on the highest-value screens
- prevent too many equally-loud cards competing for attention
- make primary actions easier to identify
- make secondary and advanced systems visible but not overwhelming
- ensure premium/store/community/world systems support the core progression fantasy rather than distracting from it

Examples:
- home should clearly answer: what should I do next?
- status should clearly answer: who am I becoming?
- store should clearly answer: what matters to buy next?
- world should clearly answer: how is my life/status changing visually?
- reward/result should clearly answer: what changed, and what should I do now?

==================================================
G. EMPTY / ERROR / DEGRADED EXPERIENCE POLISH
==================================================

Polish empty, loading, failure, and degraded states.

Requirements:
- empty states should remain useful and encouraging
- failed states should provide recovery, not dead ends
- AI-unavailable states should remain usable and understandable
- image/file/media failures should degrade cleanly
- premium/integration/community/live content failures should not poison the rest of the app
- every important screen should have at least a respectable empty/loading/error treatment

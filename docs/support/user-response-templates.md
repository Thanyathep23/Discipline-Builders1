# User Response Templates — Phase 30

## Rules
- Do not blame the user
- Do not expose internal technical details
- Do not promise what is not confirmed
- Keep tone calm and trustworthy
- Clearly state next step when possible

---

## Template 1: Issue Acknowledged

**When to use**: First response to any reported issue.

> Thank you for reaching out. We've received your report and are looking into it now. We'll update you as soon as we have more information.

---

## Template 2: Under Investigation

**When to use**: Issue requires deeper investigation; update the user on progress.

> We're actively investigating the issue you reported. Our team is reviewing the relevant data to determine what happened. We'll follow up with you once we have a clear answer.

---

## Template 3: Resolved

**When to use**: Issue has been identified and fixed.

> Good news — the issue you reported has been resolved. [Brief description of what was fixed, e.g., "Your reward has been properly credited to your account."] Please refresh the app to see the updated state. If you notice anything else, don't hesitate to let us know.

---

## Template 4: Unable to Reproduce

**When to use**: Issue cannot be reproduced on our end.

> We've looked into the issue you described, but we were unable to reproduce it on our end. This sometimes happens due to temporary connection issues or cached data. Could you try logging out and back in? If the issue persists, please let us know with any additional details (such as the exact steps you took), and we'll investigate further.

---

## Template 5: Manual Correction Applied

**When to use**: A repair or manual fix was applied to the user's account.

> We identified the issue and have applied a correction to your account. Your [balance/progression/inventory] should now reflect the correct state. Please refresh the app to confirm. If anything still looks off, let us know.

---

## Template 6: Provider/System Delay

**When to use**: An external service issue is causing delays (e.g., AI provider outage).

> We're currently experiencing a temporary delay with one of our services. This may cause [proof reviews/mission generation] to take longer than usual. Our team is monitoring the situation and working to restore normal speed. Your data is safe, and any pending actions will be processed once the service is fully restored.

---

## Template 7: Reward Review in Progress

**When to use**: User reports missing or incorrect reward; investigation is ongoing.

> We're reviewing the reward for your recent mission. We want to make sure everything is calculated correctly before making any adjustments. We'll update you once the review is complete.

---

## Template 8: Purchase Issue Being Checked

**When to use**: User reports a purchase problem (charged but item missing, etc.).

> We're looking into the purchase issue you reported. We take transaction integrity seriously and are verifying all relevant records. We'll get back to you with an update shortly.

---

## Template 9: Progression Update Delay

**When to use**: User notices a delay in level/XP/skill updates.

> Sometimes progression updates take a moment to fully sync. Please try closing and reopening the app. If your progress still doesn't look right after refreshing, let us know and we'll investigate further.

---

## Template 10: Known Incident Affecting Multiple Users

**When to use**: A confirmed system-wide issue is affecting multiple users.

> We're aware of an issue currently affecting [brief description, e.g., "proof submissions" / "the marketplace"]. Our team is actively working on a fix. We'll send an update once the issue is resolved. We apologize for the inconvenience.

---

## Template 11: Follow-up Explanation

**When to use**: User confused about follow-up flow (thinks it's a rejection).

> It looks like the system asked a follow-up question about your proof — this is not a rejection. The follow-up helps us verify your work more accurately. Simply answer the question, and your proof will be re-evaluated. You can submit up to two follow-up responses.

---

## Template 12: Dispute Acknowledged

**When to use**: User disagrees with a proof verdict.

> We understand that the verdict on your proof may not feel fair. Our review system is designed to evaluate proof quality across several criteria. We're taking a closer look at your specific case and will get back to you. Thank you for your patience.

# Progression Economy Map — v1.0

## Early Stage (Days 1-3)

### Coin Inflow
- 2-6 missions per day
- Average 40-200c/day depending on engagement
- Cumulative: 80-600c by end of Day 3

### Sink Opportunities
- Focus Trophy (80c) — immediate Day 1 purchase
- Starter Timepiece (200c) — Day 1-2
- Indoor Plant Collection (300c) — Day 2-3
- Silk Pocket Square (400c) — Day 2-3
- Starter Ride car (500c) — Day 3-5

### Save/Spend Tension
LOW — users should feel encouraged to spend early. First purchases build emotional attachment.

### Target Emotional Outcome
"I earned real rewards. I own something. My character/room is starting to look like mine."

### Biggest Risk
If rewards are too low or first items too expensive, user never reaches the "first ownership" moment and churns.

---

## Mid Stage (Days 7-30)

### Coin Inflow
- 4-8 missions per day (habitual)
- Average 150-400c/day
- Cumulative: 1000-8000c by end of Day 30

### Sink Opportunities
- Mid-tier watches (900-1200c)
- Premium Hoodie (800c), Silk Business Shirt (2800c)
- Technical Slim Trouser (1500c)
- Dark Office environment (1000c)
- Room decor (800-2800c range)
- Series M car (2500c)
- Alpine GT car (5000c) — stretch goal

### Save/Spend Tension
MODERATE — users must choose between:
- Multiple smaller items OR save for one bigger item
- Wardrobe upgrade vs. car vs. room
- This tension is the core engagement driver

### Target Emotional Outcome
"I have real choices. Some things I want are just out of reach. I need to keep going."

### Biggest Risk
- If too much is affordable: no aspiration, prestige items trivialized
- If too little is affordable: "I earn every day but can't buy what I want" → frustration

---

## Advanced Stage (Days 30-90+)

### Coin Inflow
- 5-10 missions per day (committed)
- Average 200-500c/day
- Cumulative: 5000-25000c by Day 90

### Sink Opportunities
- Royal Series watch (3500c)
- Titanium Ring (3500c)
- Milano Cashmere Coat (6000c)
- Continental S car (8500c)
- Executive Suite environment (5000c)
- Executive Suite Theme (6000c)
- Approaching: Genève Perpetual (7500c), Executive Suit (9000c)
- Aspirational: Phantom Noir (15000c), Carbon RM (18000c), Vulcan R (25000c)

### Save/Spend Tension
HIGH — users must make meaningful trade-offs:
- "Do I buy the epic watch or save for the car?"
- "Do I complete my room or chase the legendary tier?"
- Selective premium/status expression, not infinite abundance

### Target Emotional Outcome
"I've built something real. I have status items that others don't. But there's still more to achieve."

### Biggest Risk
- Power users exhaust all mid-tier content and hit a dead zone waiting for legendary items
- Solution: The gap between 5000-15000c items provides multiple intermediate goals
- Legendary tier (15000-25000c) should always feel distant but not impossible

## Stage Comparison Matrix

| Dimension | Early (D1-3) | Mid (D7-30) | Advanced (D30-90+) |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Daily inflow | 40-200c | 150-400c | 200-500c |
| Affordable items | Starters, trophies | Mid-tier wearables, first cars | Prestige wearables, premium cars |
| Save pressure | Low | Moderate | High |
| Spend satisfaction | Immediate | Regular | Selective |
| Aspiration visibility | Cars, watches | Epic/legendary items | Legendary/endgame |
| Economy risk | Dead start | Flatline or inflation | Content exhaustion |

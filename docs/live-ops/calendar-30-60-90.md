## Rotation & Retirement Rules

### What Gets Repeated
- Weekly challenges rotate through 5-6 templates (focus, discipline, skill, proof quality, purchase, streak)
- Monthly milestones follow progressive targets (Level 3 → 5 → 8 → ...)
- Comeback hooks run continuously

### What Gets Rotated
- Spotlight items change weekly/monthly
- Seasonal themes shift every 4-6 weeks
- Challenge categories cycle to prevent fatigue

### What Gets Retired
- Events with <10% participation rate after 3 runs
- Reward values that cause economy anomalies
- Themes that don't resonate (measured by engagement lift)

### What Should Be Data-Informed by Day 90
- Which event types have highest participation
- Which spotlight items drive most purchases
- Whether comeback hooks actually convert
- Whether weekly cadence is too frequent or too sparse
- Economy health during event periods

## Feature Section Intro Copy

### "How It Works" Section

**Headline**: "Four steps to real progress."

1. **Create a Mission** — Choose a focused commitment. Set your time target and category.
2. **Run a Focus Session** — Start the timer. Lock in. Do the work.
3. **Submit Proof** — Describe what you accomplished. Attach photos or screenshots.
4. **Earn Your Reward** — The AI Game Master reviews your evidence. Verified effort earns coins, XP, and character progression.

### "Why It's Different" Section

**Headline**: "Built for people who are done with fake productivity."

- **Proof-based, not checkbox-based** — You can't just tap "done." You prove what you did.
- **AI Game Master** — Adaptive missions and fair, evidence-based review. Not a chatbot — a judge.
- **Earned identity** — Your wardrobe, room, garage, and rank reflect real discipline. Core items are earned through proof.
- **Premium, not playful** — Dark, serious design for people who take their growth seriously.

### "What You Build" Section

**Headline**: "Your discipline becomes your world."

- **6 Skills** — Focus, Discipline, Sleep, Fitness, Learning, Trading. Each ranked from Gray to Red.
- **Your Room** — A Command Center that evolves from a starter studio to an executive suite.
- **Your Wardrobe** — Items earned through verified missions. Rarity from Common to Mythic.
- **Your Garage** — Vehicles that signal your commitment level. From Urban Runner to Black Signature.
- **Your Rank** — Prestige tiers (Ascendant, Apex Operator, Legion Elite) for long-term mastery.

## "What We Are / What We Are Not" Positioning Statement

### What We Are
- A Life RPG for real-world discipline
- A proof-based productivity system — every reward requires evidence
- A visible identity builder — your character, room, and world reflect real effort
- An AI accountability partner that judges fairly based on evidence
- A premium, serious tool for people who care about real growth

### What We Are Not
- A to-do list or task manager
- A casual game or entertainment app
- A habit tracker that lets you check boxes
- An AI life coach that gives advice
- A social media platform (Circles provide accountability groups, but this is not a social network)
- A pay-to-win system — core rewards are earned through verified effort
- A replacement for therapy, medical advice, or professional development programs

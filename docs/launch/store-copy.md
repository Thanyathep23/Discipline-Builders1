## Long Description

**Your life is the game. Your discipline is the currency. Your proof is the key.**

DisciplineOS is a Life RPG where your real-world effort creates real character growth. No fake checkboxes. No empty streaks. Core rewards are earned through verified evidence.

**How It Works**

Create a mission — a focused commitment with a time target. Start a focus session. When you're done, submit proof of what you accomplished. An AI Game Master reviews your evidence against mission requirements. Only verified effort earns rewards.

**Why It Feels Different**

Most productivity apps let you lie to yourself. DisciplineOS doesn't. The AI proof system means every coin, every XP point, and every level was earned through real action. You can't shortcut your way to the top.

**Key Features**

- Create missions across Focus, Discipline, Trading, Fitness, Learning, and more
- Run timed focus sessions with automatic tracking
- Submit proof of completion — text, photos, or screenshots
- AI Game Master reviews your evidence and rewards verified effort
- Earn coins and XP for every approved mission
- Level up across 6 skill categories with visible rank progression
- Customize your wardrobe, room, and garage with earned items
- Join accountability Circles to stay on track with others
- Adaptive difficulty that grows with you
- Streak tracking with streak-saving comeback system
- Quest chains for deeper skill development
- Prestige tiers for long-term mastery

**Build Your Identity**

Your character isn't just an avatar — it's a reflection of your discipline. Every item in your wardrobe, every upgrade to your room, every vehicle in your garage was earned through verified effort. Your room is your Command Center. Your rank is your reputation.

**Proof-Based, Not Performance Theater**

We built DisciplineOS because habit trackers failed us. Checking a box isn't discipline. Proving you did the work — that's discipline. The AI Game Master doesn't care about excuses. It cares about evidence.

**Start your first mission today. Prove your first day. See your character begin to evolve.**

## Feature Bullets (Benefit-First)

1. **Missions that matter** — Create focused commitments across discipline, fitness, trading, learning, and more. Every mission has a target, a time limit, and proof requirements.

2. **Proof, not promises** — Submit real evidence of completion. Text, photos, screenshots. The AI Game Master reviews your proof against mission criteria.

3. **Rewards you've earned** — Coins and XP flow from verified effort. Core items are earned through discipline, not purchased with real money.

4. **Character that evolves** — Six skills. Rank progression from Gray to Red. Visual character evolution that reflects your real growth. Prestige tiers for mastery.

5. **A world that reflects you** — Customize your room, wardrobe, and garage with items earned through discipline. Your Command Center is your identity.

6. **AI Game Master** — Adaptive missions tailored to your skills, goals, and difficulty preference. From Easy to Extreme — you choose your challenge level.

7. **Smart momentum** — Streaks that reward consistency without punishing setbacks. Comeback challenges for when life gets in the way. Focus sessions with built-in timers.

8. **Quest chains** — Multi-step mission sequences that build deeper skills. Focus Recovery, Trading Apprentice, Learning Momentum, and more.

## First-Proof Explanation Copy

### Headline Options
1. "Show what you did" (Recommended — simple, non-intimidating)
2. "Submit your evidence"
3. "Prove your mission"

### Subcopy Options
1. "Describe what you accomplished. Be specific — the AI Game Master checks your evidence against mission requirements." (Recommended)
2. "Write what you did, what you produced, and what remains. You can attach photos or screenshots too."
3. "The more specific your proof, the better your reward. The AI Game Master is fair — just be honest."

### Tone Guidance
- Do NOT say "submit evidence for review" — sounds like a court proceeding
- Do NOT say "your proof will be judged" — sounds punitive
- DO say "show what you did" — feels natural and inviting
- DO say "the AI reviews your work" — feels fair and helpful

## Comeback-Safe Phrasing

When users return after inactivity:

### Do Say
- "Welcome back. Start clean."
- "Your world is still here."
- "One session to restart."
- "No judgment. Just begin."

### Don't Say
- "You've been away for X days" (shame-inducing)
- "Your streak was lost" (punitive)
- "You missed X missions" (guilt-tripping)
- "Are you sure you want to continue?" (doubt-inducing)
